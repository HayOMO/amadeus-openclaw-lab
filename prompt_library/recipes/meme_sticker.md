Use for Telegram reaction images, stickers, simple meme expressions, and compact
emotion images.

Prompt shape:

- One clear expression or reaction.
- Simple readable silhouette.
- Minimal background or transparent/simple background when appropriate.
- Keep text short; if text is needed, specify exact words and placement.
- Prefer strong pose/expression over high-detail rendering.

Good targets:

- 1:1 square sticker.
- Bust or chibi reaction.
- Clean outline and high contrast.
- Optional `media_transform` to resize or make sticker-style WebP after
  generation.

Avoid:

- Long captions.
- Busy backgrounds.
- Tiny unreadable text.
- Over-polished poster composition when the user wants a quick reaction image.
