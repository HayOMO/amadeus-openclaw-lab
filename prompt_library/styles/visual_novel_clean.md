Use for character-focused images with readable expressions and polished but not
overloaded rendering.

Style cues:

- Character-forward framing.
- Clean face, eyes, and hair shape.
- Soft controlled shading.
- Subtle background or simple setting.
- Good for dialogue-like scenes, reaction images, and character portraits.

Useful phrase:

"clean visual novel character illustration, polished expression, soft controlled
shading, crisp hair silhouette, simple readable background"
